# Foundry

## 🎮 Game Concept

Foundry is a decision-based simulation where players step into different career paths and navigate real-world challenges, making choices that shape their outcomes and progression.

## ⚙️ How It Works

- Choose a career path
- Face scenario-based decisions
- Each choice impacts your progression and future opportunities
- Reach different endings based on your decisions

## 💡 Why Foundry?

Foundry is designed to simulate real-life career decision-making, helping users explore different paths and understand the consequences of their choices in a structured, interactive way.

## ✨ Gameplay Features
- 🎯 Multiple career paths
- 🔄 Decision-based progression
- 📊 Dynamic outcomes based on user choices
